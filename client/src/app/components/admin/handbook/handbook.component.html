Cards

Cancellations and Removal:
1.a

All cards may be cancelled if a customer wish for it to be so. you may not cancel or remove a card if a customer has not requested it via the "messages" tab and only if the request was directly to your Dizzy Admin's account.

1.b

If card is cancelled all remaining credit on that card will deduct the customers balance at time of cancellation. this process can and will not be reversed. all reversal request must be notified a general manager for further assistance.

1.c

Card removal is a permenant process and can not be reversed. a card number that has been removed will not be assigned to any user at any time.

1.d

A maximum of 3 cards can be assigned to any user at all times. if a card is not permenantly removed from the account a new card can not be registered.

1.f

Card providers are our partners. we can not control a payment that has been declined from our partner providers.

Loans

2.a

Customers are free to request a loan at any amount or spread. yet, if the customer's balance is insuficient and or you as an admin has any suspicion the customer will not be able to pay his loan DO NOT approve the loan and deny it IMMEDIATELY.

2.b

We do not require a reason for cancellation from an admin. saying so we urge you to message a user with a polite letter explaining the decision at hand and if willing the reason behind it.

2.c

Here at Dizzy we acknowledge that our customers may sometime struggle. we try to do what ever needed so no one stay at debt. if u see an insufficient balance close to the loan and payment reduction rate (the 1st of the month) we urge u to contact the customer ASAP and try to sort a payment plan.
